FAQ

Can I attend?

If you are at least 18 years old and a college student or have graduated college in the past year, then yes!

How does registration work?

Registration is open now! Click the Apply link for more details. Admissions will be based on a weighted random lottery and will be done in two waves. If you're accepted, you will have a week to confirm your attendance.

How much does this cost?

Nothing, zero! Admission is free and includes meals, drinks, snacks, overnight housing, workshops, swag, and an incredible experience!

Is there any travel reimbursement?

If you're travelling more than 30 miles to the Space Needle, then we'll reimburse your travel, as long as you upload your travel receipts upon confirming your attendence. We will reimburse up to $200 for travel within the U.S. and from Canada and Mexico and up to $500 for other international travel. If you're from Vancouver, BC or Pullman, WA - we may also provide buses to those locations, so stay tuned!

How do teams work?

Teams are at most 4 people. You can select teammates when you register, or you can register individually without specified teammates. We’ll run the lottery by teams, so rest assured you’ll be able to hack with your friends if that’s what you prefer!
